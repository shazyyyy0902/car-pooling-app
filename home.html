This application has been created to make travelling easier and much more affordable. Car pooling is sharing a vehicle with a certain number of people who are going to the same destination or who are going to alight along the way.

Fees are shared between the people in the vehicle depending on the distance they are travelling. As a member of this applicaion you are also given an option to book a vehicle for private use only.

Every member has a choice to either pick people who have requested a ride or they can request a ride for themselves. After taking a ride you can rate a driver good or bad depending on the driving if he/she causes any inconvenience.
